About the Firm

Staffing in Motion was founded by Nicole Adelsohn. Nicole started her career in finance and wealth management after studying business and receiving her Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and Chartered Investment Manager (CIM) designations. She has worked at both banking and independent Wealth Management firms. This education and experience gave her a unique perspective for recruiting in these industries. She has extensive experience working as a Recruitment Manager in Finance, Wealth Management, Capital Markets, Accounting and Sales.

Why you should work with a recruiter?

Unfilled positions result in loss of productivity, efficiency, quality and ultimately profitability. Recruiting on your own can be time consuming, frustrating and unproductive. As experienced recruiters, we can save you time. We have various recruiting tools that assist us in narrowing the search, in addition to a catalogue of numerous candidates matching different skill sets and experience. We do all the screening and only present you with the best candidates suited for the role. Your business is your specialty, recruiting for you is ours.
